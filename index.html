How It Works

Our system uses advanced machine learning algorithms to analyze images of plants and detect any diseases. Follow these simple steps:

  1. Take a clear picture of the plant you want to analyze.
  2. Upload the image to our website.
  3. Our system will process the image and provide a diagnosis.

With our user-friendly interface, you can easily navigate through the process and receive instant feedback. Our goal is to empower you with the knowledge to keep your plants healthy and thriving.
